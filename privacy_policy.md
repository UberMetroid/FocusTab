# FocusTab Privacy Policy

FocusTab is designed with your privacy in mind.

## Data Collection

FocusTab stores the following data locally on your device using Chrome/Firefox storage:
- Your daily focus task
- Your dark mode preference

**We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.**

## Data Usage

The data stored locally is used solely to:
- Remember your daily focus task between sessions
- Remember your theme preference (light/dark mode)

## Third Parties

FocusTab does not share any data with third parties.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on GitHub:
https://github.com/UberMetroid/FocusTab/issues
